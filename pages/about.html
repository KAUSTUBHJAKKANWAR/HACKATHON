About the Project

Know India is an informative and cultural web experience built as part of a Web Development Hackathon 2025. The project aims to showcase the diversity, leadership, and heritage of India using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

From the majestic Himalayas to the coasts of Kanyakumari, and from cultural traditions to modern achievements, India's story is vast and vibrant. Through this platform, we present a simple yet insightful digital journey that educates and inspires.
